Japanese

I study Japanese and am at the N4 level. I self taught for several years, before taking Japanese II at USC last semester. See the video on the right for my Japanese II final project: a day in my life.

Classics

I have a strong interest in Classics -- specifically Latin language and Roman culture. I studied Latin for six years, in middle and high school; I took up to Latin V. I took the AP Latin exam with a score of 4. I considered minoring in Classics at USC, but I wanted to have more open units for various electives. Since a computer science major took the majority of my units, not having a minor allowed me to study abroad with the extra units. I still love Classics, and have visited ancient sites like the ruins of Pompeii and Herculaneum in Naples and Knossos in Crete.

I've taken an intro course in guitar at USC, but I'm mostly teaching myself by looking at tabs of my favorite songs. I'd like to write my own, but inspiration needs to strike. Once I am proud enough with a recording, I'll post one of me playing here.
